About Travel with Maalaka

Travel with Maalaka is a trusted Sri Lanka travel agency based in Bentota, offering fully personalized private tours across the entire island. Whether you're looking for wildlife safaris in Yala and Udawalawe, relaxing beach holidays in Mirissa or Unawatuna, cultural experiences at Sigiriya and Anuradhapura, or scenic hill country journeys in Ella and Nuwara Eliya – we create the perfect itinerary for your Sri Lanka travel needs.

Starting in Bentota

Bentota's beautiful beaches serve as the perfect starting point for many of our tours. Whether you are staying locally or arriving from the airport, we make your transition to exploration seamless and enjoyable.

We pride ourselves on providing comfortable air-conditioned vehicles, an English-speaking team, and flexible itineraries. Our focus is on safety, comfort, authentic local experiences, and creating lifelong memories for every guest.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about traveling with us in Sri Lanka

What is included in your private tours?

All our private tours include:

  • Comfortable, air-conditioned vehicle with professional English-speaking driver
  • Pickup and drop-off from your hotel or airport
  • Flexible itinerary – we adjust according to your preferences and pace
  • 24/7 WhatsApp support during your trip

Not included: entrance fees to attractions, meals (unless specified), personal expenses, tips.

What is the best time for a wildlife safari in Sri Lanka?

The best time for wildlife safaris (especially Yala and Udawalawe) is:

  • December to March – dry season in the south/east, best leopard & elephant sightings
  • May to September – good for Wilpattu & Minneriya (north-central dry season)

We can help you choose the right park and time based on your travel dates.

Do I need to book far in advance?

Peak season (December–April) books up faster — we recommend 2–4 weeks in advance.

Off-season or last-minute trips are often possible — just WhatsApp us and we'll do our best to arrange something perfect for you.

Is it safe to travel with you in Sri Lanka?

Yes – safety is our top priority.

  • Professional, experienced drivers with good safety records
  • Well-maintained, air-conditioned vehicles
  • 24/7 support via WhatsApp
  • We follow all current government health & safety guidelines

Many families and solo travelers have traveled with us safely and happily.

Can I customize the tour itinerary?

Absolutely! All our tours are fully customizable.

Tell us your interests (more wildlife, relaxed beaches, cultural sites, adventure, family-friendly pace, etc.), how many days you have, and any special requests — we create a tailor-made plan just for you.
