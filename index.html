About Travel with Maalaka

Travel with Maalaka is a family-owned travel agency based in Bentota, Galle District, Southern Province, Sri Lanka, proudly owned by Maalaka Galbadage. With 7+ years of experience in tourism industry, we specialize in crafting personalized private tours across Sri Lanka – from wildlife safaris and ancient heritage sites to relaxing beaches and misty hill country tea estates. Our team of professional drivers and knowledgeable guides ensures safe, comfortable, and memorable journeys tailored to your preferences.

Starting in Bentota

Bentota's beautiful beaches serve as the perfect starting point for many of our tours. Whether you are staying locally or arriving from the airport, we make your transition to exploration seamless and enjoyable.

We pride ourselves on providing comfortable air-conditioned vehicles, an English-speaking team, and flexible itineraries. Our focus is on safety, comfort, authentic local experiences, and creating lifelong memories for every guest.
